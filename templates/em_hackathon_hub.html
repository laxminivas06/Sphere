🌐 Allow External Students
When enabled, students from other colleges can register for this hackathon. They will be able to enter their college details and student IDs manually.
📤 Project Submission Portal
When enabled, the Project Submission Portal tile becomes active. A volunteer or the team itself can enter the Team ID, auto-fetch team info, and submit the evaluation details (problem statement, solution, software/hardware type, tech stack, and presentation file) to be reviewed by evaluators.

When enabled, the Leaderboard becomes visible to participants. Keep this disabled during the hackathon and enable it only when you want teams to see the final or intermediate standings.
